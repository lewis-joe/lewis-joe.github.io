What's the point of this Portfolio?

And other important questions

"Experience with programming" can mean many things, as can "I like to code". What I mean when I say them is that I like to use coding to understand, demonstrate and navigate phenomena — ideally natural ones. If that's not clear enough I've included some projects below to explain!

Due to the application-based nature of these coding projects, it is impossible to discuss the coding without including other aspects of the projects to a certain degree. As interesting as I think these other aspects are, I have tried to only include enough information to provide context.

This unfortunately means that there is no space to discuss questions relating to such important areas of life as backpropagation in machine learning, the beautiful simplicity of the fanbeam correction in CT scans, and the structural differences between bird and human lungs. There is not even enough space to discuss the fractal nature of traditional African architecture and villages and the existential consequences this can lead to.
